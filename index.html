Our Story

Who we are and how we met

  • 2010

    How It Began

    While they grew up within the same school district and area, Jena and Troy did not meet until December of 2010, when they were introduced by mutual friend (and groomsman!), Tim Lynch, over half-priced appetizers at Applebees in West Bend. This initial meeting occurred over Jena’s winter break from University of Wisconsin-Green Bay. The newly acquainted pair did not reunite until May, when Jena returned to Slinger for Sumer Break after her second year. Upon Jena’s return, Jena and Troy worked together at OfficeMax, bonded over cookouts, beach trips, and general summer fun and quickly became enamored with each other.

  • 2011 - 013

    No Distance Too Far

    As Jena completed her Communication degree in Green Bay and Troy worked as a Network Administrator and then Software Developer in the Minneapolis area, Jena and Troy maintained a long distance relationship. The couple visited each other often and became well-acquainted with Highway 29. This time was trying for the couple, but they both agree that the experience made them and their relationship stronger.

  • 2013 - 2014

    Finding Our Dream Jobs

    After graduation, Jena spent time in both Minneapolis and Madison before returning to Green Bay for a position at her alma mater, UW-Green Bay. Then Troy moved to a new company as a software engineer still remaining in the Minneapolis area.

  • January 31st 2015

    Our Engaugement

    On a weekend visit to Green Bay, Troy proposed to Jena the morning of January 31, 2015 at the Bay Beach Wildlife Sanctuary, a place the pair both enjoy. After sharing their special moment, Troy surprised Jena with a prearranged opportunity to feed her favorite inhabitants of the sanctuary, river otters named Sadie and Sammy, their breakfast.

  • March 2015

    Finally Together

    Troy moved to Green Bay, and Jena and Troy are now happy to reside together in Bellevue, WI with their Bull Terrier, Floyd. Jena works as a Social Media Specialist at UW-Green Bay and Troy works remotely for Imagine Print Solutions as a Software Engineer.
